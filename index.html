I am Zifei SHAN (单子非), a second-year Master's student in Stanford University Computer Science.

I am doing research with Professor Christopher Ré in Stanford InfoLab. I am one of the builders of DeepDive, a scalable general probabilistic inference engine. My research aims at building and using knowledge bases, to help people better understand and exploit data.

I got my Bachelor's degree in CS from Peking University. I was also a visiting student at Technion---Israel Institute of Technology on a Research Exchange Program.

Research

Currently I am interested in knowledge base construction with joint inference. In our group we are discovering how to use structural knowledge to improve low-level computer tasks like domain-specific OCR. We are using our scalable inference engine DeepDive to empower our work.

In undergrad I was working on mining and modeling social and information networks, including ranking baseball players in a competition network, and detecting Sybil attacks in a massive online social network.
