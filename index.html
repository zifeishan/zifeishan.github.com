I am Zifei Shan (单子非). I am interested in bringing dark data to light --- extracting structured information from unstructured data.

I work at Lattice Data as one of the first engineers.

I obtained my Master's in Computer Science in Stanford University. In Stanford, I was a research assistant with Professor Christopher Ré in InfoLab. I was one of major developers of DeepDive, a well-recognized scalable general probabilistic inference engine.

I got my Bachelor's degree in CS from Peking University. I was also a visiting student at Technion---Israel Institute of Technology on a Research Exchange Program. I interened at Toshiba Research & Development Center in Tokyo, and Tableau Software.
